welcome note

On behalf of the management, staff and students of the School of Science, Oyo, I welcome yu to the official website of the great citadel. We pride ourselves in the serenity of our environment which has enabled a positive academic atmosphere in the college, our dedicated and experienced staff, never cease to impact knowledge into our ever ready enthusiastic learners. Every parent/guardian who desires a quality life for her child/ward would recognise the utmost importance of choosing the right school for her. A child needs the combination of capable and dedicated hands, an enabling environment to develop physically, mentally, socially and emotionally, which we can be found in our school. At SOS Oyo we have a comprehensive, diverse, engaging and all-inclusive curriculum that helps our students develop into academically successful and responsible individuals. This curriculum promotes the three facets of complete learning: cognitive, affective and psychomotor. We hope you find all the information useful, and it gives you an insight into our school. If you require more information, do not hesitate to get in touch with us via SOSO@gmail.com. We look forward to having a rewarding relationship with you. Welcome to our Website! Dr. (Mrs.) O.A Olabode Principal
